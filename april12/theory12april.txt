3)What is nosql?
ans.NoSQL database are non-tabular databases and store data differently than relational tables. NoSQL databases come in a variety of types based on their data model.
 The main types are document, key-value, wide-column, and graph. They provide flexible schemas and scale easily with large amounts of data and high user loads
